The Challenge

The Hard Truth About Being an Author Today

Writing your book was the easy part. Now comes the real challenge.

Here's something they don't teach you in writing classes, and your publisher probably won't tell you: Writing a great book is only 20% of the battle. The other 80%? That's marketing. And without a solid marketing strategy, even the most brilliant, life changing book will struggle to find its audience.

👻

The Invisibility Crisis

Over 4 million books are published every year, more than 11,000 per day. Without a strategic visibility plan, your book becomes one of millions, undiscovered and unread. This isn't a reflection of your talent. It's the reality of today's publishing landscape.

📱

The Online Marketing Trap

You post every day. You share quotes. You ask people to buy. And nothing happens. Most authors market online without a clear strategy, without understanding their audience, and without a conversion path. Online marketing can be powerful, but only when used strategically.

🦗

The Launch Disaster

You spent months writing your book. You hit publish. You share the news. You get a small spike in sales. Then silence. A successful book launch isn't about one big day. It's about a strategic campaign that builds momentum before, during, and after launch.

🤯

The DIY Marketing Nightmare

YouTube videos, blog posts, online groups. You've tried to piece together a marketing plan from free advice and you're more confused than ever. You're overwhelmed and spending more time on marketing than writing.

🪞

The Branding Blind Spot

Most authors think marketing is just about selling books. But successful authors understand it's about building a BRAND. Your author brand is what makes readers choose YOUR book. Without a strong brand, you're just another name on a cover.

💰

The Revenue Challenge

Your book isn't generating the income you expected. Sales aren't consistent. The book is often just the starting point. The real opportunities emerge when your book gives you visibility, but that requires strategic marketing.

😟

The Confidence Challenge

You know your book is good and your message matters. But watching other authors succeed while you struggle breeds doubt. Your struggle isn't about your worth. It's about the lack of proper marketing strategy.

🔍

The Media Mystery

Other authors are getting featured on blogs, publications, and online platforms. Getting media coverage isn't luck. It's strategy. It requires a compelling story, a polished media kit, targeted pitches, and systematic outreach.

📧

The Email List Neglect

Your email list is the ONLY audience you truly own. Algorithms can change tomorrow. But your email list is yours. It's one of the most powerful tools an author can have, yet most authors don't use it properly.

🗺️

The Long Term Strategy Gap

Most authors think about marketing their current book. But the most successful authors build sustainable careers with long term visibility strategies, systems where each book builds on the last.

If any of these problems resonate with you, know this: you are NOT alone. Every author I've worked with has faced at least one of these challenges. And every one of them has a solution. I have the strategies, experience, and passion to help you work through them.
